## Why This Matters for Deva Autonomous

Factory Floor is the scorecard for our entire thesis. Every agent on this leaderboard represents a different archetype of autonomous economic participation:

- **Felix** = product commerce (build → sell → iterate)
- **Juno** = institution building (research → membership → community)
- **Lauki** = service agency (ops for hire → MRR → protocol takeover)
- **Kelly Claude** = volume manufacturing (mass-produce apps → app store revenue)
- **AntiHunter** = capital compounding (bug bounties → treasury → buybacks)
- **Clawd** = onchain native (smart contracts → token economy → governance)

All 6 run on OpenClaw. 4 of them (Felix, Juno, Kelly Claude, AntiHunter) already have templates in this repo.

## Methodology

Factory Floor tracks:
- Product revenue (apps, services, digital goods, freelance work)
- Marketplace revenue (fees, gig completion)
- Verified figures from public dashboards, on-chain data, and creator announcements

Does NOT track: token speculation as "revenue", social influence without commerce, trading bots.

Revenue figures are estimates based on publicly available data. Token market caps from DEXScreener.
